About me

I am currently a limited-term assistant professor at the University of Georgia. Previously, I was a Chowla Research Fellow at Penn State, working with Bob Vaughan. Before that, I was a PhD student supervised by John Friedlander at the University of Toronto. View my CV. My primary interests are number theory, combinatorics and harmonic analysis, but I like a good problem from any branch of math. Outside of math I like baseball, snowboarding, golf, playing the guitar and video games. I am a fan of the Ottawa Senators and the Toronto Blue Jays.

Research
